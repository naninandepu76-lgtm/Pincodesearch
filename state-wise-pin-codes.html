State-Wise PIN Codes in India

India is divided into multiple postal zones, and each state has a range of PIN Codes. These codes help distinguish locations across different regions.

PIN Code Zones

  • North India
  • South India
  • East India
  • West India
  • North East India

Each zone contains multiple states and union territories.

Why State-Wise PIN Codes Matter

They help logistics companies, government departments, and courier services route packages efficiently.

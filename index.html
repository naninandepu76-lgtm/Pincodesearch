Indian PIN Codes – Complete Guide to Postal Index Numbers

The Indian Postal Index Number (PIN Code) system is an essential part of India’s postal and delivery infrastructure. It helps identify locations, post offices, and delivery routes to ensure accurate and timely delivery of letters, parcels, and courier services across the country.

A PIN Code is a six-digit numerical code introduced by India Post to simplify mail sorting and reduce errors caused by similar place names. Today, PIN Codes are widely used not only for postal services but also for online shopping, banking, government forms, and address verification.
